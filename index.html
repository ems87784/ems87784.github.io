Evan Sinclair Biography

Evan Sinclair

Evan Sinclair was born on November 10th, 1997, and is from Cumming, Georgia. From an early age, Evan loved sports and played it all the up until college, where he now attends The University of Georgia in Athens, Georgia. Evan's favorite sports are baseball, football, basketball, and golf. Evan played all of these sports at both Lakeside Middle School and South Forsyth High School. Evan also enjoys movies and TV shows. His favorite movie is The Dark Knight and favorite show is House of Cards. Not only does Evan enjoy sports and TV, he also enjoys being involved in extracurricular activities and clubs such as Miracle and Delta Sigma Phi Fraternity. Evan greatly believes in academics and took over 10 college-level classes in high school, and hopes to be accepted into Terry Business of College as a Finance Major.
